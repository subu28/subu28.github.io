Blood Donation Camp at IITH

Any cause is a succesful one when taken up by the cream of the country. The students of Indian Institute of Technology proved this statement right through the blood donation camp held on 8th october(Saturday)

Jointly organized by the Elan team and the NSS team of IIT H, this four hour event was an enormous success as a large crowd of students turned up to showcase their support for this social initiative.

The hospital staffs were kept busy throughout the event as more than 150 students came to donate blood. Apart from the students there were staff members as well making the event a big success.

IT was heartening to see the young minds of the country stand up for such social causes and we hope that it is just a matter of time before the other institutes follow suit.
