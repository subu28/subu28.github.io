Elektronica:

Élektronica is for all the e-enthusiasts who love tweaking with electronic gadgets. Élektronica aims at teaching and helping the interested students understand the seemingly incomprehensible electronic gadgets in the world today. It also assists students in developing devices that they conceptualize. It strives to invoke curiosity and interest amongst the students towards the field of electronics.

Students are given an opportunity to do various projects under the club and some of them, even under the mentorship of professors. Some of the projects are chosen to improve the e-infra of the Institute.
