Torque:

Are you interested in cars? or motorcycles, perhaps? For all automobile fans out there, we bring to you " TORQUE" the official automobile club of IIT Hyderabad. TORQUE is the heaven for all those who just can't know enough of automobiles and want to know more. TORQUE organizes many events like Automobile quiz ..etc to keep your knowledge of automobiles on check and also provides a lot of knowledge by conducting creative activities in this domain.
