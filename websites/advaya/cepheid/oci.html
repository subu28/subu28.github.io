Online Celestial Inquisition:

Event format :
  • There will be two phases in this event. Each phase will span four weeks. There will be 3-5 questions about astronomy in each phase and each question will carry some marks depending on its difficulty level.
  • In phase one, participants will have opportunity to win a cash price for every question.
  • The most convincing answer will be given a price of Rs. 100.
  • There will be 5 questions in phase one.
  • In phase two , each question will carry some points. At the end of 2nd phase , top two having maximum points will be chosen.
Price money for winners in phase two :
  • 1st price Rs. 800
  • 2nd price Rs. 500
Rules and regulations :
  • Interested students should participate individually.
  • Participant is allowed to use Internet or any other source available to him/her.
  • Answer should be sent within the time limit mentioned in the questions.
  • The decision of event organizer will be final.
