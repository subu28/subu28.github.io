Format for prelims:

  1. One problem statement will be posted on our website every week which will carry 50 points.
  2. Students can participate in a team of maximum 3 members.
  3. Maximum of 5 teams will be selected for the finals to be held during the fest.
  4. Teams can choose their level of difficulty and accordingly the negative points will be given:
    1. Beginner - 30
    2. Intermediary - 20
    3. Expert - 10

Format for finals:

  1. Finals will be conducted in two phases one is logical reasoning competition and the other is coding competition.
Winners will be selected entirely on the basis of their score in the finals which shall not be affected by the score of prelims.
