Automobile quiz

Students can form groups of 2 or 3.

Stage I
There will be a set of questions that are to be answered by all the groups in a paper provided to them. Each question will be weighted with some marks. There is a scope for partial marking depending on the question. There will be no negative marking in this stage.

Stage II
5 or 6 teams will be selected into the 2nd stage depending on their scores in the 1st stage. In this stage there can be 5 to 6 rounds. In each round a specific type of question set will be asked. In this stage, there might be a chance for negative marking.

Final Scores
The final score will be a cumulative score of both stages of the quiz.
