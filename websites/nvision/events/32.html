PROQUEST

1. Beginner Level
"Debugging" is an integral part of Computer Programming and those proficient in it can save themselves from a lot of headache !!
At this Beginner Level, we will be testing out the "LOGIC" in you.

Snippets of code will be given; you have to either decipher the output or sniff out the errors in the given output.

--> Only pen & paper. No laptops will be allowed !!

2. Intermediate Level
"Ready....Set....CODE !!"
At this Intermediate Level, our main aim is to test your ingenuity, patience and of course your programming skills.

The problem statement will comprise of 3-4 questions. Two will be teasers (short but tangy !!) and the others will be hard-core programs. Programming Language of your choice be it C, C++, Java, Python, etc.

--> Laptops are allowed. However internet cannot be used !!

3. Advanced Level
This round is based on deep concept in JAVA. The round consists of detection , correction and completion of code which could contain compile time errors, exceptions and some typical logical flaws.
The code in some questions can also be optimised, on doing which bonus marks are given.The round’s question can be any part of JAVA’s basic packages.

This round is a team event with at max 2 members and no laptops/digital gadgets/internet will be allowed.
