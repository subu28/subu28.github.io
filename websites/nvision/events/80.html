Electronic Bloopers

Find the bloopers and ‘debug’ it , that’s how the event goes.

Event structure
This is an event with on spot registration. The participants will form the team and play two rounds of Electronic event. The preliminary round is a quiz based. The teams selected from round 1 will participate in round 2 ,which will be a practical circuit debug round.

Time limit for first round : 30-45mins
Time limit for second round : 45-60mins

After first round only 5-7 groups(depending on number of participation) will be selected for next round.
For each round ,the team which completes the task in lesser time will be given extra scores.
Team of max. 2or3 will be allowed. Every group should contains atleast one 1st year btech
More information will be provided on the day of event. Contact organizers for the further enquiry.
