RoboSoccer

Problem statement:
All participants should make a 3 wheeler wired remote controlled Bot which can move, hold the Table Tennis ball and hit it into opponents Goalpost to score goal. By the end of the time bot scoring more Goals wins the match.

Arena specifications:

  • Field is 150cm (length)X100cm (width).
  • Field is divided into two half fields and side walls of height 10cm covered with 1 inch(2.54cm) thick thermocol sheets.
  • Goalpost of width 40cm.
  • 37.5cm Dotted line for penalty shoots.

Rules and Regulations:

  • Bot shouldn't hold the ball more than 5sec
  • Bot shouldn't damage arena and opponent's Bot (if found doing so, corresponding team will be disqualified)
  • Team of maximum 3 members
  • Total match time 3 minutes
  • Bot maximum dimensions should be 25cm X 25cm X 25 cm
  • Maximum voltage supply to the Bot is 12V
  • Only wired and manually controlled bots
  • The one who scores more Goals announced as the winner of that match
  • First all matches are knock out matches later semis and finals.
  • If Draw, then there will be a penalty shoot outs.

Penalty shoot out:
Every team has to throw the ball from the three circles which are on 37.4cm line directly into opponents Goal posts, that time opponent won't be there. There are three circles on this line. First time middle one preferred. Later, anyone in the remaining two. This will be continued until tie breaks.
