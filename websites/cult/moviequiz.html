Movie Quiz

submitted by Albin Abraham (me10b003)

The Movie Quiz was organised by Kanishta Agarwal, Sumit Jadhav and Albin Abraham, the members of Behind The Lens, on 10th Feb from 9:00 pm to 12:00 am. Eight teams of three members each participated in it.The prelims consisting of 20 questions were followed by the second round for which six teams qualified.

In the first part of this round a three-minute video was shown that had clips of sixty-three different movies. The task (no points for guessing...) was to identify the maximum number of movies. One of the teams identified astonishingly fifty-one movies. The next part was Dumb Charades, in which each group was given two chances and ninety seconds to convey the movie.

Top four teams made it to the rapid fire, the third and the final round. Teams had to answer a maximum of twelve questions in ninety seconds. Exhilarating as it turned out to be, it ended in a tie for the third position. Another rapid fire was done but the tie stood unresolved. The organisers then resolved it by asking questions related to Oscars. Thus ended the Movie quiz for the running semester, with Abhishek R and team, Piyush Naik and team, and Chitransh Chalotre and team grabbing the first, second and third positions respectively.
