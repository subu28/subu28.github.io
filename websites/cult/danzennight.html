DANZEN NITE

Y Aditya (ee11b039) and Khemchand Gujjar (ee11b011)

Danzen Nite, a night dedicated to dance exclusively, was a great idea implemented by my seniors, namely Sai Ankit and team. Dance is a form of entertainment which makes the performers and audience reach the peak of enjoyment . Thus, a separate night for dance stands justified and Danzen Nite was just as rocking as it could be.

It was organized at the institute backstage with proper sound and lighting systems already in place. Dr.Suhash Ranjan Dey had been invited to judge the event. As expected, a huge number of spectators had turned up. To start with, the solo performances stole the show with the hip hop sequences drawing whistles from the crowd. They made sure that the crowd do not go back amazed. But the highlight of the night was still in store, the group performances. Spectators cheered all the way through the songs as the Gattu guys imitated the happening Tollywood heroes to their hits. Laugh riot was a dance performance in which all the boys dressed up as girls and performed ‘body shakes’. A fourth girls' group enthralled the audience with a peppy “Sheila Ki Jawani”, whereas Mohita Ajmera mesmerized everyone with her classical performance. Amongst all these amazing performances, a group of fourth year boys distinguished themselves with their unique dance steps.

After all the performances, the judge gave away the prizes. Not surprisingly, But that was not the end. The party continued to rock with the DJ night, and so did we.
