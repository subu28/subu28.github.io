Poster Presentation

In this event, contestants prepare a poster that showcases and effectively presents experiments with given objectives, new ideas related to the field or even an entire business plan. The main idea of the event is to analyse a topic and exhibit presentation skills within the restricted space of expression. The more creative you get, the higher is your chance of impressing the panel.

Prize money of Rs.10,000/- to be won.
Participation certificates will be given to all the finalists.
