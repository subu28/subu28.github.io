Havikoro

Elan 2011 provides a treat for break dance and hip hop music fans as it proudly presents Havikoro ,a Houston, Texas-based group of dancers which specializes in break dancing, hip hop and rap music and is of immense international repute.

HaviKoro was part of the American Voices Association, promoting American music and culture abroad and some of its shows are aimed to spread awareness about drug abuse and to empower youth.

HaviKoro toured major U.S. cities and all around the world. They visited and performed in places like Finland, Paris, Frankfurt, Tokyo and London. HaviKoro has also performed onstage with artists like Destiny's Child, Limp Bizkit, Coolio, Black Eyed Peas and Planet Funk.
This show is the first of its kind in india and it is an absolute must for music and dance enthusiasts. YO!!
