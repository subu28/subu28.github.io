Fire on Dawson

Fire on Dawson is an Indo/German rock band from Karlsruhe, Germany, formed in 2006.

It consists of founder and bassist Martin Sonntag, guitarist Markus Stricker and Indian vocalist Ankur Batra.

Initially, starting out as an experimental funk metal project, the band has over time, developed a unique alternative/progressive rock sound, combining the various musical and cultural influences that the different band members have brought along with them. The sound of the band is characterized through their songwriting play, with songs usually starting out clean, with lush guitar parts that suddenly explode into thunderous, distorted choruses. This, coupled with the vocal and lyrical ability of front man Ankur Batra, has placed Fire on Dawson at the forefront of their genre in the German music scene, which till now had mostly been dominated by pop-punk and party-alternative acts.

Their recent full length venture ‘Prognative’ has received much critical acclaim.

Their albums so far include:
• Seven Symptoms EP (2007)
• Prognative (2010)
