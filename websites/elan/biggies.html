BIGGIES

As the sun sets and stars light up the sky, tunnels and trenches cave out and whispers and rumours are heard throughout the woods. The coyote's night call for more numbers could be heard from far away. Slowly and slowly as groups gather and the numbers rise rapidly, a slight presence of unearthliness could be felt. Gently, all eyes focus on the sudden brightness erupting from the midst of the thickets and all move forward to witness the spectacle, the marvel, something that hasn't taken place for many centuries.

Truly BIGGIES are the part of ELAN where everything gets BIG... Best Performers, Best bands, large audiences and perfect setting. Biggies are the happening moments of ELAN. With shows like PRO-Nite, GEAR-UP and RAMPAGE, ELAN is sure to be a part of your memory forever.

Biggies make sure that no-one leaves the place without drowning themselves in enjoyment. So this January, behold 'Biggies' at Elan 2k11, where adrenaline levels skyrocket and the volume is cranked up to the max.
