Hack-O-Mania

Are you fascinated on hearing the word hacking? Do you want to protect your private data? Or Both? We at Elan 2011, in collaboration with i3indya technologies, provide you the perfect opportunity to learn the nuances of hacking (ethical!) through Hack-O-Mania.

This workshop, on ETHICAL HACKING AND CYBER SECURITY, is not only designed to enlighten you on the basics of ethical hacking (yes, you WILL be able to hack into your college server at night!!) but also to expand your knowledge on cyber security. So fasten your seat belts and get ready to share your space and time with other hacking enthusiasts and cyber-crazy guys from all over the country.

Workshop was held on 23rd and 24th october 2010.
