Electronic Bloopers

Do you like meddling with circuits? Do you like tinkering with the circuits in electronic items? Do you like designing circuits but are just plain lazy to make a complete one yourself? Then you are at the right place.

Elan 2011 gives you the opportunity to present your logic, your circuit designing skill (and your laziness) here at Electronic Bloopers. Join in this fun and exciting event as you race against time to ‘Debug’ the given circuit

Prizes worth Rs 6000/- to be won

All you need to do:

All you need to do to participate in this event is, form a team of 2 or 3 and then register for this event via the website of Elan .A small questionnaire will be presented before you to answer and the teams will be selected based on their answers.
