Be dazzled by the amount of sheer wit and spontaneity in the room. A minute never did seem this entertaining. Eight people are given a minute of fame and only one person can speak at a time... And interestingly, people hate sharing their minute
So be utterly bamboozled as you die laughing or crying as the incredible speakers of the royal language fight it out, trying not to stutter,stammer, pause unnaturally, make grammatical errors or repeating anything...However they fail miserably...
The Bottom Line: Speak fluently in flawless Queen's English as long as you can. Find flaws in your opponents' speech.
