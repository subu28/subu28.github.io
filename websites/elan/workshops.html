Workshops

Are you looking for an immensely enjoyable yet an educative experience? Or is it interaction with esteemed personalities from various academic fields that you are after? If yes is the answer to at least one of the above questions, then head straight to the workshops at Elan, where we provide interesting sessions of entertaining yet highly educative lectures.

We at Elan are combining the famous "watch and learn" and "do it yourself" ideologies by roping in illustrious personalities who are experts in their fields, and also providing rich hands-on experience during the lectures under their guidance..

The workshops promise to be an enriching and enjoyable experience for the participants with loads of interaction expected among the different people sharing similar interests.
