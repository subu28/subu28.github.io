RatRace is an effort to efficiently distribute information about different career options available in this world. It is a humongous task that we want to accomplish with the help of people already in the profession. We believe talking to a person who has seen it and done it, can offer useful insights on the topic. After all, it’s the first-hand experience we never get to know. We, in our endeavour are trying to easily present the nitty gritties of a lesser known career to all of you. We are also trying to help you take a well informed decision by making sure you have access to both true and updated information. We are also trying to bring forward lesser known opportunities to everybody's knowledge.

John F. Kennedy once said, "Things do not happen. Things are made to happen." And we are there just to help you do that. Everybody is unique. Everyone should get a chance to realize his uniqueness and excel at it. People have done it and we can learn from their experiences, both good and bad. With us, you'll never run another ratrace, because with RatRace you'll play your natural game!

If you chose to travel the road less travelled, We would like to talk to you! Drop a line at ratrace@ratrace.in
