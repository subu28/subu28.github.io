Akash Gautam - Soft Skill Sensei

Akash is a "Power Speaker". He is Chief Motivator and Trainer at Akash Gautam Training Solutions. Popularly known as Edutainer, he has successfully mixed entertainment and education with his unique style of humour and presentation. His clientele includes many well known names of the corporate industry. His job has taken him from suave ballrooms of five star hotels to villages of Haryana. He is deeply passionate about youth issues and has several unique offerings for them too. He has more than 10 years of first hand experience with Soft Skill management. His Facebook community, Akash Da Vocab Dhaba is the largest community in Asia dedicated to Vocabulary training.

As our Soft Skill Sensei, Akash is available to all RatRace Readers and Fans to answer their queries.

Madhu Chadda - Soul and Mind Sensei

Madhu is a Counselor, Trainer, Consultant, Teacher, Philanthropist and a Filmmaker. In a career spanning more than 30 years, she has changed numerous lives with her healing touch. She understands young minds like no other. She has worked with CMC, TCS, Computer Point, Satyam Computer and ICICI Bank before changing her focus to counseling. She has also actively set up and managed several NGOs like Roshni and Sahaaya. Her Entrepreneurial flare has charmed us all too. She currently runs her own production house, Sat Rang Productions.

Madhu, as our Soul and Mind Sensei, will guide us on our path to soulful wellness. She is available to all RatRace Readers and Fans to answer their queries.
