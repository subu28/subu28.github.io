Tips For Writers - sharing, content, keywords

  • Be Real - Write about stuff you really beleive in. This will make your article believable and enjoyable.
  • Be Honest - All articles are peer rated. So, make sure your article is relevant and updated. Take extra care to validate the facts.
  • Be Smart - Share and publicise your article to increase your chances of getting into Top 10. Write a relevant "title" and file your article under proper categories
  • Be Helpful - Give positive feedback, rate others' articles and share your ideas

Top 10 Article

  • All the articles will be peer reviewed. Only members of Community who have submitted atleast one article can rate the article of a fellow community member.
  • Articles not rated atleast once will be rated by Editor of Community.
  • This will carry 50% of total weightage. The other half will be given on basis of no. UNIQUE page views.
  • The Top 10 list will be dynamic. It can change anytime.
  • The top 10 articles at the last day of the month (Final Top 10) will be given a token of monetary appreciation from RatRace.in in accordance to payment policy.
  • Final Top 10 articles will not particpate in any ranking in future.

Featured Articles

    Community Editors will pick a maximum of 5 articles each month based on quality, relevance and maturity of article.

Payment Policy

  • Syndicated articles will be paid 20% of the amount earned as a token of appreciation from RatRace.in.
