Ink Spot

Ink Spot is a forum of ideas for RatRace.in readers to exchange opinions, ideas and tips on subjects ranging from career opportunities to the business of life. We encourage you to participate by writing articles and poems; and sharing, discussing and critiquing each others’ work.

Ink Spot Rules

We want everyone to benefit from this community of thoughtful peers and therefore request that you contribute thoughtful and sincere comments and content. However, there are certain mandatory rules that each person ("Member") participating in this Community must follow.

Each Member must comply with the following rules of conduct while participating in this community:

  • You must use either your Pen Name (as registered with RatRace.in) or your actual first and last name when you participate in this community, including when posting any comments or participating in any discussions.
  • You may not represent that you are any other person, whether real or invented, or imply any connection with any person or organization with which you are not in fact associated.
  • You must demonstrate appropriate respect for other Members. If you disagree with an opinion, feel free to challenge that opinion with a posting of your own. You must not personally attack any other Member. You will not stalk or harass another Member.
  • You will not upload, post or submit anything that is obscene or contains profanity or that may be hateful or offensive on racial, ethnic, sexual or any other grounds; is harmful, vulgar or distasteful; or is defamatory, libelous, or invades another person's privacy or proprietary rights.
  • You will not use this community for illegal purposes.
  • You will not send any bulk unsolicited advertising, promotional information, commercial email or other solicitation (including without limitation junk mail, "spam," chain letters or pyramid schemes of any sort) to any person through the use of this Community.
  • You will not upload, post or submit anything that contains any viruses, cancel bots, Trojan horses, harmful code, or other computer software or program designed to interrupt another person's enjoyment of this Community.
  • You will not upload, post, submit or otherwise make available any content that you do not have a right to make available or that infringes any patent, trademark, trade secret, copyright or other proprietary rights of any person.
  • You will not upload, post or submit or otherwise make available any content that contains links to Web sites with objectionable content, excessive links to external Web sites.

We reserve the right to remove any content at any time from this Community, including without limitation if it violates the above rules. We ask that you report content that you in good faith believe violates the above rules to moderator@RatRace.in

If any Member violates any of the above rules, we reserve the right to temporarily or permanently ban that individual from further participation in RatRace.in and/or Community.

Messages may be removed if they contain:

  • advertisements
  • profanity
  • links to Web sites with objectionable content
  • excessive links to external Web sites
  • personal attacks directed at another reader
  • "spammed" material that has been posted in multiple forums
  • comments that aren't relevant to the forum's topic
  • comments with objectionable images attached

Profiles

Your profile identifies you to other RatRace.in readers and Community participants. You should create a profile to make it easier for community members to connect with you. It also gives readers a greater sense of your perspective when they read your posts. Adding a photo puts a face to your name. All your posts will be accompanied by your name and photo to credit you as well as establish the source of post.
